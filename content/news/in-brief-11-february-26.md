# In brief | 11 February '26

**Reuters:**[**German auto industry in 'crisis' as investments, jobs move abroad, lobby says**](https://www.reuters.com/business/german-auto-industry-crisis-investments-jobs-move-abroad-lobby-says-2026-02-10/)****

"Germany is experiencing a huge crisis as a business location," says car industry association VDA.

**Politico:**[**European chemical giants plot to weaken EU's flagship climate policy**](https://www.politico.eu/article/european-chemical-giants-weaken-eu-flagship-climate-policy/?)

The ultra-polluting sector says the EU's carbon price is putting it out of business.

**Bloomberg:**[**EU should soften caps in emissions market, key lawmaker says**](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-02-10/eu-should-soften-caps-in-emissions-market-key-lawmaker-says?sref=peEFYOHm)****

ETS reform should involve slower pace of emissions reductions for industries, said EPP environmental lead Peter Liese.

**AFP:**[**Solar, wind capacity growth slowed last year, Global Energy Monitor analysis shows**](https://www.yahoo.com/news/articles/solar-wind-capacity-growth-slowed-034400659.html)****

Announcements and construction starts grew 11 percent, following an increase of 22 percent in 2024, casting doubts on the goal of tripling renewable capacity by decade-end.

**Reuters:**[**Britain secures record amount of solar in renewable power auction**](https://www.reuters.com/business/energy/britain-secures-record-amount-solar-renewable-power-auction-2026-02-10/)****

A total of 6.2 gigawatt (GW) of onshore wind, solar and tidal power projects won contracts, with the bulk, some 4.9 GW, going to solar.

**Reuters:**[**France to invest €50 mln in Imerys lithium project**](https://www.reuters.com/business/energy/france-invest-50-mln-euros-stake-imerys-lithium-project-2026-02-11/?)****

Investment in country's flagship project for the battery metal will help cover feasibility studies before final investment decision.

**Times of India:**[**Reducing China dependence: India eyes critical minerals deals with France, Brazil, Canada, says report**](https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/articleshow/128159024.cms?)

According to sources, India is looking to replicate elements of an agreement it signed with Germany in January.

**Bloomberg:**[**Banks backing green steel fund ‘false solutions,’ report says**](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-02-11/banks-backing-green-steel-fund-false-solutions-report-says?sref=peEFYOHm)

Almost all major banks that have pledged to finance low-carbon steel are backing initiatives that will lead to additional emissions, says nonprofit BankTrack.

**Bloomberg:**[**Trump to direct Pentagon to buy coal power to revive industry**](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-02-11/trump-to-direct-pentagon-to-buy-coal-in-bid-to-revive-industry?sref=peEFYOHm)

Trump plans to use government funding and Pentagon contracts to sustain US coal-fired power plants.

**The Guardian:**[**Some of world’s oldest trees hit by climate-fuelled wildfires in Patagonia**](https://www.theguardian.com/world/2026/feb/11/climate-fuelled-wildfires-patagonia-argentina-chile-oldest-trees)****
