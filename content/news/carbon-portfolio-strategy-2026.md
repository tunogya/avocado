Enterprise buyers are shifting from single-instrument procurement to portfolio-based carbon strategies.

## Key signal

Teams that combine long-term PPAs with shorter REC windows can reduce exposure to market shocks while keeping annual targets measurable.

## Practical move

Build a quarterly review cadence and rebalance project mix by region, grid intensity, and contract maturity.
