Liquidity does not only affect pricing. It changes how quickly teams can execute procurement mandates.

## Why it matters

Low-liquidity corridors create execution delays and force wider pricing bands.

## What to track

- Bid-ask spread trends
- Average time-to-close by region
- Counterparty concentration
