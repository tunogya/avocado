Regulatory readiness is increasingly cross-functional.

## Core checklist

1. Confirm disclosure standards in each operating market.
2. Align accounting treatment for certificates and offsets.
3. Document auditable approval flow.

Well-defined controls reduce cycle time and post-signing risk.
