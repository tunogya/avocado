# In brief | 12 February '26

**Bloomberg:**[**Merz says Europe should be open to revising carbon market**](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-02-11/merz-says-eu-should-be-open-to-revising-carbon-market?sref=peEFYOHm)

**Energy Storage News:**[**‘Moment of truth’: The 2026 regulatory agenda for large battery storage in Germany**](https://www.energy-storage.news/moment-of-truth-the-2026-regulatory-agenda-for-large-battery-storage-in-germany/)****
